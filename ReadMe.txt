An An Advanced Real-Time Job Recommendation System and Resume Analyser :
-->An advanced real-time job recommendation system and resume analyzer leveraging state-of-the-art 
Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) technologies. The system seamlessly 
integrates automated resume analysis, utilizing NLP for key information extraction and semantic 
analysis, offering actionable suggestions for content optimization. Simultaneously, the real-time job 
recommendation system employs dynamic algorithms to match candidate skills with available job 
opportunities, delivering personalized recommendations based on evolving market trends. The 
platform ensures proactive job seeking with real-time alerts for relevant positions and conducts skill 
gap analysis, empowering candidates to enhance their profiles. By amalgamating AI and NLP, this 
solution represents a transformative approach to talent acquisition, providing an intelligent and 
streamlined mechanism for job recommendation and resume enhancement.
